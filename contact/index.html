FAQ

Common questions

Do you work with international clients?

Yes — 100% remote. I work with clients in the US, Canada, Australia, and Europe. I'm flexible on meeting times to suit different timezones.

What does a typical project look like?

Discovery call → written proposal with scope and timeline → staged delivery with regular check-ins. You see progress throughout — not just a final drop.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. You describe the problem and the outcome. I handle the how. Everything is documented so your team can understand and manage it.

How do you charge?

Fixed-price for defined scopes, hourly or retainer for ongoing work. We'll figure out what makes sense for your situation on the first call.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. Most projects include a post-launch support window. Monthly retainers are available for ongoing maintenance and new workflow builds.

Not sure what you need?

That's what the Systems Audit is for. I map your stack, find the bottlenecks, and deliver a prioritized roadmap — so you know exactly what to fix first.
